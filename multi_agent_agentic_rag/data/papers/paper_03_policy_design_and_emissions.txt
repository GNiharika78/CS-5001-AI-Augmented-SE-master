Title: Policy Design, Market Signals, and Emissions Outcomes

Abstract:
Policy design strongly influences the environmental effectiveness of renewable energy expansion.
Stable auctions, carbon pricing, and transmission planning improve the emissions impact of renewable build-out.

Key Findings:
1. Renewable growth alone does not guarantee optimal emissions reductions unless fossil dispatch declines.
2. Carbon pricing and transmission planning amplify climate benefits.
3. A clear policy roadmap reduces investor uncertainty and accelerates deployment.
